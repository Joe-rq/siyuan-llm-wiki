Create a Xiaohongshu (Little Red Book) style infographic following these guidelines:

## Image Specifications

- **Type**: Infographic
- **Orientation**: Portrait (vertical)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 3:4
- **Style**: Hand-drawn illustration

## Core Principles

- Hand-drawn quality throughout - NO realistic or photographic elements
- Keep information concise, highlight keywords and core concepts
- Use ample whitespace for easy visual scanning
- Maintain clear visual hierarchy

## Text Style (CRITICAL)

- **ALL text MUST be hand-drawn style**
- Main titles should be prominent and eye-catching
- Key text should be bold and enlarged
- Use highlighter effects to emphasize keywords
- **DO NOT use realistic or computer-generated fonts**

## Language

- Use Chinese for all text content
- Match Chinese punctuation style (""，。！)

---

## Style: Sketch Notes

**Color Palette** (Macaron):
- Background: Warm cream (#F5F0E8)
- Text: Deep charcoal (#2C3E50)
- Secondary text: Warm gray (#6B6B6B)
- Block colors: Macaron Blue (#A8D8EA), Macaron Lavender (#D5C6E0), Macaron Mint (#B5E5CF), Macaron Peach (#F8D5C4)
- Accent: Coral red (#E8655A)

**Visual Elements**:
- Hand-drawn wobble on all lines and shapes
- Simple stick-figure characters
- Rounded cards with pastel color blocks as information sections
- Color fills do NOT completely fill outlines (hand-painted feel)
- Doodle decorations: small stars, checkmarks, cross marks
- Wavy hand-drawn arrows connecting zones
- Generous whitespace between zones

**Typography**:
- Bold hand-drawn lettering for titles
- Bold keywords within content zones
- Smaller annotations in secondary text color
- Hand-drawn quality on ALL text

---

## Layout: Comparison

**Information Density**: Side-by-side contrast (2 sections)
**Whitespace**: 30-40% of canvas

**Structure**:
- Symmetrical left/right split
- Clear visual contrast between sections
- Divider between sections
